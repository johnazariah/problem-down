# Chapter 06: The VQE Pipeline

_This chapter pairs with the previous application chapter. It teaches the algorithm from first principles._

## In This Chapter

- **What you'll learn:** The complete VQE pipeline — from molecular integrals to fermion-to-qubit encoding to Pauli measurement to classical optimisation.
- **What you need:** This chapter builds on Chapters 2 and 4, where we introduced qubits, gates, and phase-based computation. Here we add fermions, second quantisation, and the measurement overhead problem — because electrons aren't qubits.
