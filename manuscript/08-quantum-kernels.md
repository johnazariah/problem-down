# Chapter 08: Quantum Kernels in Detail

_This chapter pairs with the previous application chapter. It teaches the algorithm from first principles._

## In This Chapter

- **What you'll learn:** How to build a quantum kernel classifier — feature map design, kernel estimation circuits, and when quantum kernels provably outperform classical ones.
- **What you need:** This chapter builds on the gate vocabulary from Chapters 2 and 4. The key new idea is the Born rule as a kernel evaluator — measurement probability as similarity.
